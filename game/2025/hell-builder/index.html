Hell Builder – Roguelike City Builder

Strategic Building Meets Infernal Synergy

Release: Aug 6, 2025  |  Status: Upcoming

Hell Builder is a roguelike city builder where every structure placement shapes the path to victory. Strategically lay down hellish buildings from a deck of cards to earn Souls, unlock new structures, and eventually fulfill the Dark Lord’s infernal vision.

Master synergy chains to optimize your domain. When your resources run dry, Transmute your hellscape into powerful relics and start fresh—with progress that lasts. It’s a quick-session strategy loop with puzzle-like placement and permanent upgrades.

🃏 Core Features

  • Synergy-driven building placement
  • Dynamic unlocks based on building adjacency
  • Transmute failed builds into relics for progression
  • Pixelated aesthetic with minimal color palette
  • Quick, one-hour play sessions with no-run-wasted design
