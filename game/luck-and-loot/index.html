Luck & Loot – Dice-Building Roguelike

Upcoming Indie Game – Releasing Aug 1, 2025

Status: Upcoming

Luck & Loot is a fast-paced dice-builder roguelike where you forge and customize dice, collect powerful relics, and survive deadly dungeons filled with cursed monsters and dark secrets. Each face of the die is a choice. Every roll shapes your fate.

With pixel art, chiptune vibes, and tactical combat, it’s a nostalgic yet brutal twist on the roguelike formula — packed with item synergies, difficulty modifiers, and deep replayability.

  • 🧊 4 customizable dice per run

  • 🧠 20+ powerful dice upgrades & 40+ relics to combine
  • 👹 40+ enemy types including formidable bosses

  • 💀 High risk, high reward choices in every roll
