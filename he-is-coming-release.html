🎮 He is Coming – Official Release

Released: July 17, 2025
Developer: Chronocle
Publisher: Hooded Horse
Steam: https://store.steampowered.com/app/2824490/He_is_Coming/
Launch Sale: 35% Off – $9.74 (Ends July 31)

Deep in the forgotten corners of the world, a dark force stirs — the Demon King rises once more. He is Coming is a strategic roguelite RPG autobattler where you must stand firm against the apocalypse. Seek powerful artifacts, outmaneuver unique enemies, and adapt constantly to the threats that evolve with every day passed.

🧭 Explore, Equip, Survive

By day, you scour ruined lands for hidden weapons and rare magical items. By night, terrifying bosses begin their hunt. With over 350 items, 30+ rotating elite enemies, and deep synergies, each run demands planning and improvisation. How will you counter the Razorclaw Grizzly’s armor-piercing rage? Or the Black Knight’s ability to absorb your strength?

Carefully manage limited inventory space and long-term risk. Powerful loot can win today’s fight but doom you in the next. Each choice echoes through your journey toward the Demon King.

Key Features:

  • Over 350 items and synergies
  • 30+ unique bosses with special mechanics
  • Turn-based autobattler combat with roguelite progression
  • 100+ unlockable challenges and permanent upgrades
  • Asynchronous PvP mode: Kingmaker

🏆 Kingmaker Multiplayer

Once unlocked, Kingmaker lets you face off against builds crafted by other players, testing your wits and endurance against unpredictable combinations. This async mode rewards strategy, adaptability, and tactical mastery.
